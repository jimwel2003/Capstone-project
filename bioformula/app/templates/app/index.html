About Badang ni Ignacio

Badang ni Ignacio is run by Christian Life Community (CLC) and is accredited by Agricultural Training Institute (ATI) as a learning site for agriculture. It is an 11-hectare farm engaging both in plants and animals and is complete with facilities to provide students all the trainings they need in agriculture. To learn and adopt topics on agriculture and crop science, organic and sustainable farming, swine farming, poultry farming, making of organic fertilizers, crop protection and management.
